About

I'm a fourth-year PhD student in the Information and Language Processing Systems group at the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Maarten de Rijke and Hinda Haned. My primary research interests include interpretability and fairness in machine learning. I'm also interested in deep learning (specifically graph neural networks), ensemble methods, and AI for social impact. I completed my BSc and MSc, both in mathematics, at McMaster University in Hamilton, Canada. I also worked in credit risk analytics for a few years before starting my PhD.
