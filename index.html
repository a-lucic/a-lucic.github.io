About

I'm a Research Fellow at the Partnership on AI and a PhD student at the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Maarten de Rijke and Hinda Haned.

My research interests include explainability and fairness in machine learning, specifically for ensemble methods and graph neural networks.

I completed my BSc and MSc, both in mathematics, at McMaster University in Hamilton, Ontario. After that, I worked in credit risk analytics for a few years before starting my PhD. I grew up in Canada but was born in the former Yugoslavia.
