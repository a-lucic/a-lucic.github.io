About

I'm a fourth-year PhD student in the Information and Language Processing Systems group at the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Maarten de Rijke and Hinda Haned. My primary research interests include interpretability and fairness in machine learning. I'm also interested in deep learning (specifically graph neural networks), ensemble methods, and AI for social impact. Before starting my PhD, I worked in credit risk analytics for a few years after completing my BSc and MSc in mathematics.
